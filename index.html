Chicken Nuggets Today!

A Children's Book By Carol Kohring

About

This is a fun book written about chicken nuggets. Almost everyone loves chicken nuggets. Let's join the chicken nuggets on a magical, musical journey as they fly, skate, and climb into our hearts and tummies. And don't forget to look for the tiny turtle hidden in the pictures of the book.

About the Author

Carol Kohring

Carol is a retired instructional aide from the public schools in West Chester, Ohio. She holds a Masters of Science Degree in Corrections. She is married and has 6 grown children and 9 grandchildren. Carol has been battling Stage IV Metastatic Breast Cancer since 2018, which is treatable but not curable. Writing a children's book has been very therapeutic and enjoyable. She has been able to put the words into a song that you are welcome to join in singing it along with her. She hopes you and your little ones enjoy reading this book as much as she has enjoyed creating it.
